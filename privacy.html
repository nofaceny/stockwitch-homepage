Introduction

Abonglab ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and services (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy applies to all Abonglab apps, including but not limited to Stockwitch and future products we release.

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account or use our Services, we collect:

  • Email Address: Required for account creation and authentication
  • Username: Your chosen display name for community features
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely for email-based authentication
  • User Preferences: Language settings, region settings, and app preferences
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you choose to provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our apps, including features used, screens visited, content viewed, and time and date of activities
  • Device Information: Device type and model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and app version
  • Push Notification Tokens: Device tokens to send you notifications
  • Analytics Data: We use analytics services to collect anonymized data to improve our Services
  • Crash and Error Data: We collect crash reports and error logs to improve app stability

Information from Third-Party Services

If you sign in with third-party authentication (e.g., Google), we receive your email address, account ID, and basic profile information. We do not store your third-party passwords or access your accounts beyond basic authentication.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Provide and Maintain Our Services

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Deliver personalized content and features
  • Enable social and community features
  • Send notifications and updates

Improve and Optimize

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Identify and fix bugs and technical issues
  • Conduct product research and development
  • Test new features and improvements

Communicate with You

  • Send important service announcements
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Provide updates about our apps and services
  • Send notifications based on your preferences

Ensure Security and Safety

  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Monitor for security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our Services:

  • Cloud Services: Database and authentication services
  • Analytics: Product insights and usage analytics
  • Error Tracking: Crash reporting and error monitoring
  • Authentication: Third-party sign-in services
  • Notifications: Push notification delivery

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

With Other Users

When you use community features in our apps, certain information may be visible to other users, such as your username, posts, comments, and public activity. Profile visibility varies by app.

For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests, enforce our Terms of Service, protect rights and safety, or detect and prevent fraud.

Business Transfers

If Abonglab is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. You will be notified of any such change.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with Services. You may request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us.

After account deletion, your personal information will be deleted within 30 days, though backup copies may persist for up to 90 days. Some data may be retained longer if required by law.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and Portability: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated personal data
  • Opt-Out Rights: Disable analytics tracking, unsubscribe from notifications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@abonglab.com

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for users under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country where data protection laws may differ. If you are located outside the United States and choose to use our Services, your information will be transferred to the United States. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: Passwords are hashed and salted
  • Access Controls: Strict limits on who can view your data
  • Security Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for threats and vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or integrations. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Push Notifications

We may send you push notifications. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

Analytics and Tracking

We use analytics tools to improve our Services. You can opt out of analytics tracking in app settings. We do not currently display advertisements.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, right to delete, and right to opt-out. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@abonglab.com

European Economic Area (EEA) Rights

If you are in the EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Contact dpo@abonglab.com for GDPR-related inquiries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in our apps, updating the "Last Updated" date, and sending notifications for material changes.
